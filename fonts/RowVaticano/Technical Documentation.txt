
**Technical Documentation - Row Vaticano**

---

**1. Overview of OpenType Features**

**Row Vaticano** includes several advanced OpenType features that add versatility and enhance the aesthetic appeal of your designs. These features include:

- **Ligatures**: Automatically replaces certain letter combinations (like "ff" and "fi") with custom ligatures for smoother letter flow.
- **Swashes**: Adds decorative strokes to specific letters for a more elegant look.
- **Stylistic Sets**: Offers alternative styles for select letters, enabling you to create unique typographic effects.

---

**2. Character Set and Multilingual Support**

Row Vaticano supports a wide range of languages, including (but not limited to):
- **Western European Languages**: English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, etc.
- **Central European Languages**: Polish, Czech, Hungarian, Croatian, etc.
- **Extended Character Set**: Includes accented characters, diacritics, and symbols for full language support.

For a complete list of supported characters, please refer to the "Character Map" section in this document.

---

**3. Accessing Special Glyphs**

##### **In Adobe Illustrator**
1. Select the **Type Tool** and type your text.
2. Open the **Glyphs Panel** (Window > Type > Glyphs).
3. Use the panel to select swashes, ligatures, or stylistic alternates by clicking on the desired glyph.

##### **In Adobe Photoshop**
1. Type your text and highlight it.
2. Open the **Character Panel** (Window > Character).
3. Click the small menu icon at the top-right corner of the panel and select **OpenType** options to enable ligatures or stylistic sets.

##### **In Microsoft Word**
1. Highlight the text you wish to style.
2. Go to the **Font Settings** (under the Home tab).
3. Open the **Advanced** tab and enable **Ligatures** or other OpenType features, if available.
