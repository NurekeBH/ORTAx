Font Name: RowVaticano
Version: 1.0
Release Date: November 2024

Description:
Row Vaticano is a clean and minimalistic sans-serif font designed to bring a sense of ease and sophistication to any project. With its relaxed and friendly look, Row Vaticano is perfect for brands seeking an approachable yet modern aesthetic. Its balanced letterforms and soft edges make it suitable for a variety of design applications, from branding and packaging to digital media.
